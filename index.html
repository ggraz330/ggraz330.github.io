STEM


STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics in an academic setting.
There is a shortage of workers and adequate education in these areas, which is why I created this application to demonstrate how Technology can simplify our life and create a safe experience.


Virtual Wallets


Allow you to store credit and debit card information into an application that can be used online or on a mobile device to pay for products and services without ever taking out your credit card.

Creates a secure way to shop by not giving merchants or people around you the opportunity to acquire your credit or debit card information.

NFC
Near Field Communications

Utilizes a virtual wallet application along with an NFC chip, included in most mobile phones today, that communicates through RF to make payments. You simply open the app and tap the phone to the scanner at the register.
